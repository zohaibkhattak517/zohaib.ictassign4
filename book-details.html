The Alchemist

Author: Paulo Coelho

Rating: 4.5/5

Description: A philosophical book by Paulo Coelho about a young Andalusian shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding treasure. The story teaches the importance of following one's dreams and listening to one's heart.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Rating: 5/5

Description: A classic novel by Harper Lee that explores the serious issues of racial inequality and the loss of innocence in the small town of Maycomb, Alabama, during the 1930s.

1984

Author: George Orwell

Rating: 4.9/5

Description: A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society where the government, led by Big Brother, monitors every aspect of life. It explores themes of surveillance, propaganda, and individual freedom.

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Rating: 4.8/5

Description: A story of the excesses and illusions of the Jazz Age, centered around Jay Gatsby's mysterious life and his obsession with Daisy Buchanan.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Rating: 4.7/5

Description: A romantic novel by Jane Austen that explores themes of love, social standing, and misunderstanding between Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy.
